Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This mobile application (“the app”) allows users to search for movies, view details and trailers, and organize their selections into lists using a genre-based wheel interface. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Data Collection

The app uses third-party services provided by Google Firebase, which may automatically collect limited information:

  • Firebase Analytics – to understand how users interact with the app (e.g., which screens are visited, searches performed, genre filters used).
  • Firebase Crashlytics – to detect and fix app crashes or bugs.

The types of data that may be collected include:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data (screen visits, button clicks, search behavior)
  • Anonymous identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)

No personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, phone number, or address) is collected by the app.

How Data Is Used

The data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To improve the app’s performance and stability
  • To better understand user behavior and enhance the user experience

Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with third parties, other than through the services mentioned above (Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics).

Data Storage

Data is securely stored by Firebase. For more details, please refer to Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any significant changes will be reflected on this page.
